Camper Water System & Power Monitoring Unit

A software application that monitors the voltage the power supply, battery, gives to the Raspberry Pi, monitors the water levels of the fresh water, grey water, and black water tanks, and displays the usage of the tanks and battery over time.

Technology

The Camper Water Monitoring System & Power Monitoring Unit was built with eric IDE, Qt Designer, and RaspberryPi . The MariaDB stores water information every ten seconds, to store and keep track of water usage. A Pi-Plate DAQC card intakes information from three pressure sensors located in three different water tanks, and an Arduino that is attached to an alarm.

Hands Up

An Android mobile application that automates a user’s attendance record based on their android device’s location. It also allows a Professor to check the attendance record of their class.

Technology

Hands Up was built with Android Studio and uses the Kotlin programming language. Integrating with the Google Maps API for GPS coordinates of user’s location. Firebase database real-time provides the database solution for the storage of attendance records in Firebase's cloud.
